Technology can enable both emissions reductions and/or increased emissions (Chapter 16). Governments 27
play an important role in most major innovations, in both ‘technology-push’ (Mazzucato 2013) and induced 28
by ‘demand-pull’ (Grubb et al. 2021a), so policy is important in determining its pace, direction, and 29
utilisation (Roberts and Geels 2019a; Sections 1.7.1, 1.7.3). Overall, the challenge will be to enhance the 30
synergies and minimise the trade-offs and rebounds, including taking account of ethical and distributional 31
dimensions (Gonella et al. 2019). 32
