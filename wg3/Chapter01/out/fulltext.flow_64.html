FAQ 1.1 What is climate change mitigation? 35
Climate change mitigation refers to actions or activities that limit emissions of GHGs from entering the 36
atmosphere and/or reduce their levels in the atmosphere. Mitigation includes reducing the GHGs 37
emitted from energy production and use (eg. that reduces use of fossil fuels) and land use, and methods 38
to mitigate warming eg. by carbon sinks which remove emissions from the atmosphere through land 39
use or other (including artificial) mechanisms (See section 12.3, 14.4.5; see WGI for physical science, 40
and Chapter 7 for AFOLU mitigation). 41
