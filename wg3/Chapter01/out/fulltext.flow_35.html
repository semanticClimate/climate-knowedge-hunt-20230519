Drawing from the scenarios database, five Illustrative Mitigation Pathways’ (IMPs) were defined for this 16
report (Figure 3.5 in Chapter 3 and Table 1.1). These are introduced here, with a more complete description 17
and discussion provided in Section 3.2.5 of Chapter 3. These IMPs were chosen to illustrate key themes 18
with respect to mitigation strategies across the entire WG III assessment. The IMPs embody both a storyline, 19
which describes in narrative form the key socio-economic characteristics of that scenario, and a quantitative 20
illustration providing numerical values that are internally consistent and comparable across chapters of this 21
report. Quantitative IMPs can be associated directly with specific human activities and provide a 22
quantitative point of reference that links activities in different parts of socioeconomic systems. Some parts 23
of the report draw on these quantitative scenarios, whilst others use only the narratives. No assessment of 24
the likelihood of each IMP has been made (as they reflect both human choice and deep uncertainty). 25
