Dynamic efficiency and uncertainty 33
Care is required to clarify what is optimised (Dietz and Venmans 2019). Optimising a path towards a 34
given temperature goal by a fixed date (e.g. 2100) gives time-inconsistent results backloaded to large, 35
last-minute investment in carbon dioxide removal. ‘Cost-effective’ optimisations generate less initial 36
effort than equivalent cost-benefit models (Gollier 2021; Dietz and Venmans 2019) as they do not 37
incorporate benefits of reducing impacts earlier. 38
